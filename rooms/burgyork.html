The Burgess YorkShire Room

$249 - $329 per night

The Burgess YorkShire Room is located in the Carriage House, just steps away from the Main House. It features a king-sized bed, a full-size sleep sofa for up to two extra guests, a gas fireplace, and a spacious bathroom with an oversize whirlpool tub and separate shower. Overlooking the garden and fish pond, the room is a peaceful retreat for a romantic respite or a quiet family getaway.

Common Areas and Features Include:
  • Study with phone and DVD library
  • Large living room with fireplace
  • Dining room with fireplace, library, and guest computer
  • Deck overlooking gardens, complete with goldfish pond
  • Adirondack chairs and porch rockers
  • Self-serve coffee and tea 24/7
  • Complementary sherry or port
  • Guest refrigerator
Amenities:
