Relax & Refresh at Our Bed & Breakfast!

All six air-conditioned rooms have queen- or king-size beds with luxurious linens, cozy sitting areas, and a private bathe en suite complete with fresh flowers, chocolates, roses, and a basket of amenities. Each has a fireplace or stove, and several have whirlpool bathes. The common areas invite you to relax by the fire, savor a glass of sherry or port, and visit your friends or fellow guests.

The main house and the carriage house each have a variety of room styles to fit your needs, whether you're looking for a romantic and cozy room for two or family accommodations for up to four.
